Space Bananas

Use the mouse to move the ship up and down. Survive as long as you can by dodging space junk! You get points based on how much space stuffs pass you. (1 Piece of junk = 1 point) You have three lives. Shift + green flag to take on TURBO MODE!!! Thanks KingOfAwesome58219 and Airy_Gary. I got this idea because I like bananas. Based on Galastroid. My first remix.

ArchitectMan

The pictures (except for ArchitectMan and the table) and music are not mine. I made this for the Scratch design studio 'Create a Superhero'.

Loot

Press the spacebar to start. Go around the maze collecting money. There are 4 levels. Try not to go off the path! You have 3 lives. Konkalootoomps are the currency of loot. The lowest box is your Konkalootoomps. I made this project for a school fete.

SlideCross

Press the spacebar to start. Use the left and right keys to switch between sliders. Use the up and down keys to move a slider. The aim is to get all the sliders to the top hitting the horizontal slider as little as possible. The box on the far right is the amount of times you have hit the horizontal slider. After 150 seconds the game will automatically end. Please ignore the gray writing on the end screen. I made this project for a school fete.

Castle Robber

Move your mouse around the screen. Get the robber to the key without him hitting the electric bars. My first ever project on Scratch!
