NaturalBuilder v0.03 ☞DRAFT☜

This is only a very basic prototype of a sort of building thing. I will continue it some time. Instructions: Click on a block to remove it (dig mode) or change it (build mode), Press D to go onto dig mode, Press B to go onto build mode (default), In build mode press 1-3 to change the block type (3 is default), Press U to display your username (default), Press N to choose a custom name, Press X to not display any name. AT THE MOMENT ON BUILD MODE YOU CAN ONLY CLICK ON EXISTING BLOCKS.

SelfieMaker

To save the selfie: Click this link, Click 'See inside', Right click on the stage (the big window in the top left corner), Click 'save picture of stage, Select the file destinaton (and name if you want) and click 'Save'. FUNCTIONS: Press 'm' to select the moustache, Press 's' to select the sunnies, Press 'b' to select the beard, Press the arrow keys to move the selected item, Press '0' to make the selected item dissapear, Press 1-3 to change the look of the selected item, Press 'n' to display your usename in the selfie, Press 'x' to hide your usernname, Press 'i' and 'k' to change the filter (filters 1-7 are the same colour but different exposure - 1=brightest 7=darkest. Personally I use 7, but it varies in different light), Press 'p' to hide the variables 'Selected' and 'Filter' so it is not in your selfie, Press 'd' once you have taken your selfie and want the 'Selected' and 'Filter' variables back. YOU NEED A WEBCAM FOR THIS TO WORK.

SlideCross

Press the spacebar to start. Use the left and right keys to switch between sliders. Use the up and down keys to move a slider. The aim is to get all the sliders to the top hitting the horizontal slider as little as possible. The box on the far right is the amount of times you have hit the horizontal slider. After 150 seconds the game will automatically end. Please ignore the gray writing on the end screen. I made this project for a school fete.
